Mi Biblioteca

This is a mobile application developed through Kotlin and the Android Studio IDE, carrying out the authentication and management of databases through Firebase with the CRUD model and the use of APIs to access the user's location. Its function is to carry out detailed monitoring of the books that the user has read, loaned or reserved and to access the general information of the libraries closest to their current location; achieving friendly access and appropriate use of data and multimedia files by the user.

SACHA

Design and implementation of the "Sistema avanzado de clasificación de huevos automático" (SACHA), through Arduino, with which it has been possible to reduce work time in several local sheds, thus creating the possibility of increasing the productive capacity of small poultry farmers in the region. Currently, the system is being developed to keep the product accounting.

  • Arduino
